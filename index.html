Vermont's Historic Sites

The State of Vermont has 212 historic markers in their online database, but less than half have recorded latitude and longitude. I used data from Wayfaring.com, the Historical Marker Project, and Google Maps imagery to attempt to geolocate the entire set.

There are definitely some interesting patterns here-- five markers in Woodstock, none in Barre-- but I'll save that for future analysis.
